About HenryCosmetics

Welcome to HenryCosmetics, where your beauty transformation awaits. Nestled in the heart of Ottawa, our salon began as a small dream in the vibrant mind of our founder, Henry, a dedicated beautician with a passion for making people feel and look their best.

Our Story: It all started in 2014, when Henry decided to transform his passion for cosmetics and beauty care into a tangible reality. With a flair for the artistic and a keen eye for the latest trends, Henry opened the doors to HenryCosmetics, a place destined to become more than just a salon. From the beginning, Henry's vision was clear: to create a welcoming space where anyone could step in and leave feeling beautiful and confident. Henry's charisma and expertise quickly attracted a team of like-minded professionals, each skilled in different aspects of beauty care. Together, they expanded the salon's offerings. What began with basic hair and makeup services soon included advanced skincare treatments, luxurious spa therapies, and state-of-the-art aesthetic enhancements.
